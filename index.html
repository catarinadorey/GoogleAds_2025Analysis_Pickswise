Google Ads Performance Analysis – Pickswise

The comparative analysis of the Google Ads account performance reveals a significant improvement in traffic acquisition efficiency at the beginning of October compared to September. However, this top-of-funnel efficiency has not translated into higher-value conversions, with conversion rates for Signups and First-Time Deposits (FTDs) remaining stagnant. The most critical issue is brand performance: Hard Rock Bet drives the majority of signups but has generated zero FTDs, whereas BetMGM shows a much stronger ability to convert users into depositors.

Brand Performance

The Critical Issue: Lead Quality by Brand

The analysis by brand is the most critical finding. It reveals a major discrepancy in the final quality of acquired traffic.

  • Hard Rock Bet: Accounts for 64% of signups in September and 78% in October, but converted zero of these 86 signups into FTDs.
  • BetMGM: Despite generating fewer signups, it is the only brand consistently generating FTDs, demonstrating much higher lead quality.
