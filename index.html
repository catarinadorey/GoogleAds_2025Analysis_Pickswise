Performance Analysis – Google Ads (September vs. October MTD 2025)

Executive Summary

The analysis of data from October 1-12, 2025, compared to the full month of September 2025, reveals an improvement in traffic acquisition efficiency for the landing page. In October, the Cost per Landing Page Click (LP Click) was lower, and the Conversion Rate for this action was higher.

However, a full-funnel analysis shows that the conversion rates for the subsequent steps (from LP Click to Signup and from Signup to FTD) remained constant between the two periods. A significant concentration of Signups is observed in a single brand which, in the analyzed periods, recorded no FTDs (First Time Deposits).
