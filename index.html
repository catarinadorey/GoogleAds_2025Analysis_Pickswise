Casino Strategy

September: Launch of dual campaigns per state (MI, NJ, WV, PA) targeting Generic Casino and Slots/Spins. Optimization focused on pausing low-performing keywords and CPA stabilization.

October: Paused WV due to high spend without returns. Expanded MI, NJ, and PA with new Generic terms, excluding focus on welcome bonuses/free spins to improve intent.

November: Consolidated daily budget to €300. Paused "Free Spins" and "No Deposit Bonus" keywords to test FTD conversion quality over volume.

December (1-15): Transitioning to a Lead Gen strategy with a dedicated Landing Page. Implementation of automated email CRM sequences to be tested across all states.

Sportsbook Strategy

December (1-15): Launch of three distinct campaign types:

  • Sports Generic
  • Welcome Bonus Intent
  • Top Sites & How-To Guides

Targeting Indiana, Michigan, New Jersey, Pennsylvania, Tennessee, and Virginia. Currently in the Learning Phase to establish performance baselines.
