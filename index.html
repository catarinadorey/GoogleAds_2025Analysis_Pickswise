Insights & Performance Analysis

Campaign & Region Efficiency

Campaigns focused on Michigan (MI) and Pennsylvania (PA) consistently outperform New Jersey (NJ) in both efficiency and cost control.

  • Top Performer (MI & PA): In October, MI – Generic Casino achieved an exceptional 51.61% conversion rate with a $3.98 cost per conversion.
  • NJ Underperformance: New Jersey campaigns consistently show the highest CPAs. In September, NJ – Casinos reached $22.55 per conversion, while PA and MI campaigns remained below $7.00.

Spins & Slots Segment

The Spins & Slots segment showed mixed results during the period.

  • In December, PA – Spins & Slots became the highest investment campaign ($981.32 spend).
  • Despite the scale, performance was inefficient, with a $14.65 cost per conversion, indicating diminishing returns.

Keyword Performance

Keywords related to Free Spins and Bonuses dominate both volume and apparent efficiency.

  • "free spins" is the strongest volume driver. In October, it generated 339 conversions with a $7.58 cost per conversion. In November, it reached a 25.95% conversion rate.
  • "casino bonus" showed consistent performance and became the highest spend keyword in December ($644.16).

Quality & Inefficiencies

Despite high conversion volumes, several keywords fail to generate real business value.

  • Keywords containing "free spins" and "no deposit" generate Google Ads conversions only (LP clicks), not real FTDs.
  • Inefficient terms:
    • "casino online" – $19.42 cost per conversion (September)
    • "free casino games" – $68.84 cost per conversion (September)
  • Generic Success: The Generic Casino ad groups in MI and PA delivered excellent results in October, confirming strong non-bonus intent.

Brand Performance & Revenue Quality

Key Revenue Contributors

Revenue generation is highly concentrated across a small number of brands, with FanDuel and BetMGM emerging as the most impactful partners despite relatively low signup volumes.

  • FanDuel: Delivered the strongest single FTD value in December, generating $350 in revenue from just one depositor.
  • BetMGM: Consistently produces high-value FTDs, including $472.59 in September and $164.75 in October, validating strong player quality.

Revenue Volatility & Risk Signals

Several brands show negative or unstable revenue despite generating FTDs, highlighting the importance of prioritizing net value over volume.

  • Inconsistent Signup Quality: High signup volumes (e.g. Hard Rock Bet in October with 83 signups) did not translate into proportional FTDs or revenue.
